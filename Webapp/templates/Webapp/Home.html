Machine Learning Algorithms

The idea of utilizing the power of machine learning algorithms on actual patient symptoms in a bulk to extract the predictions out of it which lead to better decision making in a such a critical scenario.

Different algorithms are working together in the mean we refer to as “Voting mechanism” Results from different algorithms are compare and vote for the match the one with the highest votes will be selected as the final prediction.

Our system have been trained on different combinations of datasets by using different cross validation techniques in order to be more accurately predict the disease that definitely result in greater satisfaction by the users.
